One-Time Setup
  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com and create a free project.
  2. Inside the project, click Build → Realtime Database and create a database (start in test mode).
  3. Click the gear icon → Project Settings → scroll to Your apps → click Add app → Web. Copy the firebaseConfig object.
  4. Open both tryout_evaluation.html and tryout_master.html in a text editor, find FIREBASE_CONFIG near the top of the script, and paste in your values.
  5. Share tryout_evaluation.html with your coaches (email the file). They open it in any browser, enter their name, and score away.
